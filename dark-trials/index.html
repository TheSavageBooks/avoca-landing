The Vickie Chronicles: Dark Trials

A paranormal southern gothic mystery.

“Hooked me immediately; couldn’t stop reading.”

Three cases. Three dark corners of the system. One girl who won’t break.

Dark Trials is the explosive prequel to The Vickie Chronicles, following Vickie Bartolucci, a fearless, quick-witted New Yorker, as she’s forced to survive three escalating mysteries, each more dangerous than the last.

From Queens’ criminal underworld… to a secluded convent hiding a murder… to a brutal juvenile facility crawling with corruption…

Vickie must outthink killers, institutions, and her own past — or disappear.

Start where the story turns dark.
