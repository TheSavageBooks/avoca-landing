Dark Trials is the explosive prequel to The Vickie Chronicles, following Vickie Bartolucci, a fearless, quick-witted New Yorker, as she’s forced to survive three escalating mysteries.

From Queens’ criminal underworld… to a secluded convent hiding a murder… to a brutal juvenile facility crawling with corruption…

Vickie must outthink killers, institutions, and her own past — or disappear.
