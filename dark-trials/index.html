A Vickie Chronicles Novel

From bestselling author I. Savage comes a fast-paced crime novel about loyalty, buried secrets, and the cost of standing up when no one else will.

Vickie Bartolucci doesn’t look for trouble.

Trouble finds her.

A hidden cassette tape drags her into Queens’ criminal underworld.

A secluded convent retreat turns into a murder investigation wrapped in silence and lies.

Framed for a crime she didn’t commit, she’s locked inside juvenile detention and uncovers a conspiracy powerful adults are desperate to protect.

Each case raises the stakes.
Each mistake carries a cost.

And every time someone tries to silence her, she pushes harder.

Dark Trials stands on its own while setting the stage for The Vickie Chronicles.

Perfect for readers who love the twist-driven pace of Harlan Coben and the addictive tension of Freida McFadden.
