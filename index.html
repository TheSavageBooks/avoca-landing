The Freaks of Avoca Island: THE STORM

A Southern Gothic mystery with paranormal elements.

“Hooked me from the beginning; couldn’t stop reading.”

From bestselling author I. Savage comes a dark, cinematic mystery set in the Louisiana bayou, where crime families rule and secrets refuse to stay buried.

FBI agent Lawrence Mayfield wakes up in a grave on a forgotten island.

He claws his way out of the mud with six bullet wounds and no memory of who ordered his execution. The storm has washed out the only road off Avoca Island. The bridge is gone. The phones are dead.

Someone on this island wants him silenced.

The LaRue dynasty controls everything. Smuggling routes. Money. Loyalty. Freedom. Mayfield infiltrated their operation. Now they know exactly who he is.

But Avoca Island is not just a crime scene.

An abandoned carnival rots in the swamp, home to the descendants of the island’s former freak show. A Keeper claims the island protects its own balance. Locals whisper that some debts are paid in blood.

As floodwaters rise and buried secrets surface, Mayfield must uncover who betrayed him before the island finishes what the grave began.

Because on Avoca Island, the storm is only the beginning.

Perfect for readers who love layered crime families, buried secrets, and Southern set mysteries with a touch of the uncanny.
