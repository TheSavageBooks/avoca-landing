Lightning splits the bayou. An FBI agent wakes up buried alive on an island that shouldn’t exist.

Lawrence Mayfield claws out of the mud with six bullet wounds and no memory of who ordered his execution. The only road off Avoca Island has washed out. The bridge is gone. The phones are dead.

The LaRue dynasty controls everything. Smuggling routes. Money. Loyalty. Freedom. Mayfield infiltrated their operation. Now they know exactly who he is.

But Avoca Island is not just a crime scene. An abandoned carnival rots in the swamp. A Keeper claims the island protects its own balance. Locals whisper that some debts are paid in blood.

On Avoca Island, the storm is only the beginning.
