The Freaks of Avoca Island: THE STORM

A paranormal gothic southern mystery.

"Hooked me from the beginning. Couldn't stop reading."

Book one is a complete, fast read—you won’t be left hanging.

Lightning splits the bayou. An FBI agent wakes up buried alive on an island that shouldn’t exist.

Lawrence Mayfield crawls out of the mud with six bullet wounds, no memory of who put him there, and no way off Avoca Island. The only road in has washed out. The only bridge has closed behind him.

Locals whisper about the LaRue dynasty—smugglers, killers, kings of the swamp. But it’s the other things that haunt the island: carnival ruins that still breathe, a Keeper who claims the island keeps its own balance, and secrets that refuse to stay buried.

The Storm is a fast, bingeable crime-gothic series.
