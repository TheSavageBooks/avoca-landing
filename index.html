Lightning splits the bayou. An FBI agent wakes up buried alive on an island that shouldn’t exist.

Lawrence Mayfield crawls out of the mud with six bullet wounds, no memory of who put him there, and no way off Avoca Island. The only road in has washed out. The only bridge has closed behind him.

The LaRue dynasty controls everything. Smuggling routes. Money. Loyalty. Freedom. Mayfield infiltrated their operation. Now they know exactly who he is.

But Avoca Island is not just a crime scene. An abandoned carnival rots in the swamp, home to the descendants of the island’s former freak show. A Keeper claims the island protects its own balance. Locals whisper that some debts are paid in blood.

As floodwaters rise and buried secrets surface, Mayfield must uncover who betrayed him before the island finishes what the grave began.

Because on Avoca Island, the storm is only the beginning.
