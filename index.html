The Freaks of Avoca Island: THE STORM

A Southern Gothic mystery with paranormal elements.

“Hooked me from the beginning; couldn’t stop reading.”

Lightning splits the bayou.

Lawrence Mayfield claws his way out of the mud with six bullet wounds, no memory of who put him there, and no way off Avoca Island. The only road in has washed out. The only bridge has closed behind him. The phones are dead.

The LaRue dynasty controls everything. Smuggling routes. Money. Loyalty. Freedom. Mayfield infiltrated their operation. Now they know exactly who he is.

But Avoca Island is not just a crime scene. Carnival ruins rot in the swamp. A Keeper claims the island protects its own balance. Locals whisper that some debts are paid in blood.

As floodwaters rise and buried secrets surface, Mayfield must uncover who betrayed him before the island finishes what the grave began.

Because on Avoca Island, the storm is only the beginning.
